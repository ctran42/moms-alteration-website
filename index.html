Serving Arlington Since 1988

Mom's Alterations is a family owned business that has been serving Arlington for more than 30 years. Located next to Arlington High School, Mom's handles all types of alteration services from wedding dresses to sewing patches. We pride ourselves on our fair pricing, quality work, and fast workspeed. Wedding, Formal, Casual, or Athletic-- We Do It All!

At Mom's, we strive to meet all your alteration needs. We can adjust your everday pants, shirts, dresses, blouses, skirts, and shorts; we can sew on patches and cloth; we can fix tears and holes; we can do prom dresses, suits, tuxedos, wedding dresses and more! Not sure if we can do it? Come on in for a consultation, completely free of charge! We'll take a look at what you have and assess if we can provide you a satisfactory service.

Why Us?

We are passionate about we do, focusing on not just delivering a great service, but also connecting with our customers. Being able to understand our customers motivates us to continue delivering the best service we can. Our walls are decorated with signed portraits of satisfied clients throughout the years, serving as a reminder of why we do what we do.
